#. one
#. two

(i) one

a. one

### extension-definition_lists

Example:

Term 1

:   Definition 1 with *inline markup*

Term 2 with *inline markup*

:   Definition 2

    ```
    some code, part of Definition 2  
    ```
    Third paragraph of *definition 2*.

Term 1

:   Definition
with lazy continuation.

  Second paragraph of the definition.

Term 1
 ~  Definition 1

Term 2
  ~ Definition 2a
  ~ Definition 2b
